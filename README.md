# Honda Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
The **Honda Sales Analysis Dashboard** is a professional Power BI project designed to track the sales performance and financial health of Honda’s core motorcycle portfolio. By visualizing metrics for key models like the **Hornet 2.0, CB350, Unicorn, SP 125, Activa 6G, and Shine 125**, this tool provides real-time visibility into profitability, regional demand, and customer satisfaction.

It empowers department managers and operational staff to move beyond fragmented reporting and make **data-driven decisions** to optimize inventory and sales strategy.

---

##  Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
-  **Power BI Desktop** – for data visualization and interactive UI design.
-  **Excel/CSV** – used as the primary data source.
-  **UI/UX Design** – implemented a custom dark-themed interface for enhanced readability and executive appeal.
-  **File Format** – `.pbit` (Power BI Desktop File).

---

##  Data Source
**Source:** Sales dataset representing Honda motorcycle transactions across various Indian states.

The dataset includes:
- **Product Details:** Model names (Hornet 2.0, CB350, Unicorn, Activa 6G, Shine 125, SP 125), segments, and colors.
- **Geographic Data:** Sales distribution across states (Delhi, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh) and cities.
- **Financial Metrics:** Net sales, gross profit, cost price, insurance amounts, and finance amounts.
- **Customer Insights:** Average customer ratings and payment methods (Cash, UPI, Credit Card, Finance).

---

##  Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem  
Management often faces a **lack of real-time visibility** and difficulty in tracking fragmented KPIs across a diverse motorcycle portfolio. Without quick insights, identifying underperforming regions or understanding the impact of financing on total sales becomes time-consuming.

### • Goal  
To deliver a **high-impact analytical tool** that:  
- Provides an instant snapshot of total orders and profitability.
- Compares performance across six specific Honda models.
- Analyzes regional sales disparities and customer payment preferences.
- Offers a seamless drill-down from high-level summaries to granular transaction details.

### • Walkthrough of Key Visuals  
- **Interactive Visual Slicer (Top Section):** Custom image-based buttons to filter the dashboard by motorcycle model.
- **Executive KPIs:** Total Orders, Avg. Customer Rating, Total Insurance/Finance, Gross Profit, and Net Sales.
- **Financial Trends (Line Charts):** Monthly tracking of Gross Profit, Net Sales, and Cost Price to identify seasonality.
- **Market Distribution (Donut Charts):** Regional analysis by State and a breakdown of Payment Categories.
- **Details Grid View:** A comprehensive table on a dedicated page for deep-dives into specific city and segment-level data.
- **Navigation UI:** Dedicated buttons for seamless switching between "Overview" and "Detail" views.

### • Business Impact & Insights  
-  **Real-Time Visibility:** Eliminated manual reporting by providing an instant view of sales health.
-  **Profitability Tracking:** Clearly identifies which models drive the highest margins.
-  **Operational Efficiency:** Allows staff to quickly identify high-demand states for better inventory allocation.
-  **Customer Behavior:** Insights into the reliance on Finance and Insurance help refine financial service offerings.

## How to Use
1. Download the `.pbit` file from this repository.  
2. Open it in **Power BI Desktop**.  
3. Use the **Visual Model Slicer** at the top to filter data for specific bikes.
4. Click the **Navigation Buttons** to switch between the Summary and Detail views.
